### 2. LOOCAFE MODEL

**Overview:**
- **Founded:** 2018 by Abhishek Nath
- **Model:** Shipping container public toilets + attached cafe/point of sale
- **Gates Foundation:** Exclusive partner for public toilets
- **Locations:** 200-400 structures (sources vary)
  - User mentioned 270+ - likely accurate for Hyderabad
  - Total India: 400+ structures across 8+ states
- **Recognition:** UNDP best practice, WASH Stewardship Award 2022

**Social Impact:**
- **Employment:** ~1,000 people employed through LooCafe model
- **Worker Dignity:** 28 street hawkers turned toilet entrepreneurs (documented in book)
- **Sustainability:** Revenue from cafe sustains free toilet access
- **Success Stories:**
  - Kalavathi - found dignified employment
  - Vijay Kumar - toilet entrepreneur
- **Business Impact:** Rs 18 crore turnover in 2 years

**How It Works:**
1. Public toilets built from shipping containers
2. Attached cafe/kiosk for revenue
3. Cafe revenue sustains free/low-cost toilet access
4. Creates employment for local entrepreneurs
5. IoT-enabled monitoring (ReFlow Toilets technology)
6. 24/7 accessibility
