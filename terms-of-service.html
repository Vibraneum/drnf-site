2. About DRNF

Dr. Rabinder Nath Foundation is a non-profit community service organization founded in 2020, dedicated to:

  • Operating free chai counters for the homeless and underprivileged
  • Community support programs and donation drives
  • Sanitation and education workshops
  • Environmental conservation and animal welfare
  • Creating meaningful employment opportunities
