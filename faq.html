Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DRNF and our community programs

About DRNF

What is Dr. Rabinder Nath Foundation (DRNF)?

Dr. Rabinder Nath Foundation (DRNF) is a community service organization based in Hyderabad, India. Founded in 2020, we are dedicated to serving the community through multiple initiatives including free chai counters, sanitation workshops, environmental conservation, and community support programs. We honor the legacy of Dr. Rabinder Nath through compassionate service.

When was DRNF established?

DRNF was founded in 2020 with a mission to serve the community with compassion and care, continuing the legacy of Dr. Rabinder Nath.

What areas does DRNF serve?

We primarily serve Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Our free chai counters and community programs are strategically located across the city to maximize impact and reach those who need it most.

Free Chai Counters

Where are the free chai counters located in Hyderabad?

We currently operate 2 active free chai counters in Hyderabad through our partnership with LooCafe. The counters are strategically placed to serve the community effectively. For specific locations, please contact us via WhatsApp.

How many cups of chai have been served?

We've proudly served over 1,16,800 cups of tea to the community! This number continues to grow as we expand our reach and serve more people every day.

How does DRNF's partnership with LooCafe work?

Our free chai counters are implemented through our strategic partnership with LooCafe.com, leveraging their extensive network of 270+ locations across Hyderabad. We successfully piloted this program across 30 LooCafes in 2022-2023 and plan to expand further across India.

Who can access the free chai counters?

The free chai counters are open to everyone in the community, with a special focus on serving those in need. Our mission is to provide warmth, comfort, and a moment of respite to all.

How many jobs have been created through this initiative?

Our free chai counter initiative has created 70+ jobs, providing sustainable employment opportunities to members of the community while serving a greater purpose.

Volunteering & Support

How can I volunteer with DRNF?

We welcome volunteers! You can join us by:

  • Contacting us via WhatsApp
  • Emailing us at info@drnf.org
  • Visiting our Contact page for more information

Volunteer opportunities include free chai counter operations, community support programs, educational workshops, environmental initiatives, and animal welfare activities.

What volunteer opportunities are available?

We offer diverse volunteering opportunities:

  • Free Chai Counter Operations: Help serve tea and interact with the community
  • Community Support Programs: Assist with donation drives and community outreach
  • Educational Workshops: Support sanitation and hygiene education
  • Environmental Initiatives: Join tree plantation and conservation drives
  • Animal Welfare: Participate in rescue and care activities

Can businesses or organizations partner with DRNF?

Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with businesses and organizations. We currently partner with Ixora Group, LooCafe, and Kadak Chai. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.

Programs & Initiatives

What other programs does DRNF run besides free chai counters?

Beyond free chai counters, DRNF runs several impactful programs:

  • Community Work: Support programs for those in need
  • Sanitation Workshops: Education on hygiene and sanitation practices
  • Environmental Conservation: Tree plantation and cleanup drives
  • Animal Welfare: Rescue and care for stray animals
  • Educational Workshops: Skill development and awareness programs

Does DRNF work with orphanages or other NGOs?

Yes! We actively collaborate with other organizations and have supported orphanages like Abhi Sai Datta Trust, especially during the pandemic. We believe in collective impact through partnerships.

Are there plans to expand DRNF's programs?

Yes! We plan to expand our Free Chai Counter program across LooCafe's network of 270+ locations in Hyderabad and eventually across India. We're also continuously developing new initiatives to serve the community better.

Funding & Operations

How is DRNF funded?

DRNF operates on a self-sustained model with support from strategic partners including Ixora Group, LooCafe, and Kadak Chai partnership. This sustainable approach allows us to focus on long-term community impact.

Is DRNF a registered nonprofit organization?

Yes, Dr. Rabinder Nath Foundation is a registered nonprofit organization (NGO) based in Hyderabad, committed to transparent and accountable community service.
