## User Journey & Conversion Points

```
Entry Point: Hero Section
    ↓
First Impression: Trust badges build credibility (3 partners)
    ↓
Engagement: Animated stats show impact scale
    ↓
Connection: Stories of Impact build emotional resonance (6 testimonials)
    ↓
Validation: Theory of change explains how impact happens
    ↓
Decision Point 1: Recent Impact Updates prove active operations
    ↓
Action: Support Our Mission Section (featured donation card with urgency)
    ↓
Conversion: "Donate Now" CTA with emotional appeal & transparency
    ↓
Secondary Actions: Food donation, newsletter, stay in touch
    ↓
Engagement: Media & social proof links
    ↓
Sharing: Social share buttons amplify mission
```

---

## Trust-Building Architecture

```
First Visit (0-15 seconds):
├─ Hero image + headline = Emotional connection
├─ Primary CTA (Support Now) = Action opportunity
└─ Urgency text = Motivation for immediate action

Early Engagement (15-45 seconds):
├─ Trust badges = Institutional credibility
└─ Animated stats = Impact scope proof

Mid-Content (45-120 seconds):
├─ Testimonials = Social proof from stakeholders
└─ Recent impact updates = Proof of ongoing work

Decision Phase (2-5 minutes):
├─ Theory of change = Logic & structure
├─ Cost transparency = Financial trust
└─ Enhanced donation section = Multiple CTAs

Post-Decision (5+ minutes):
├─ Media coverage = External validation
├─ Newsletter signup = Ongoing engagement
└─ Social sharing = Organic reach
```

## Key Conversion Metrics to Track

1. **Hero CTA Clicks**: "Support Our Mission Now" button usage
2. **Donation Section Engagement**: Card interactions and CTA clicks
3. **Testimonial Engagement**: Scroll depth to testimonials section
4. **Recent Impact Views**: News ticker section visibility
5. **Secondary CTAs**: Food donation, newsletter, stay in touch
6. **Social Shares**: Share button clicks
7. **Scroll Behavior**: Depth of engagement with page
8. **Time on Page**: Overall engagement duration
