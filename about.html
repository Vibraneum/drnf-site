Our Story

A vision of compassion, a legacy of service, and a commitment to community

Founder's Vision

The Dr. Rabinder Nath Foundation was founded in 2016 by Abhishek Nath, driven by a deeply personal mission to honor the memory and continue the legacy of his father, the late Dr. Rabinder Nath.

Growing up witnessing his father's unwavering commitment to community service, Abhishek understood that true impact comes not just from good intentions, but from creating sustainable systems that serve people with dignity and compassion.

"My father believed that no act of kindness, however small, is ever wasted. He spent his life serving others, and through this foundation, we ensure that his spirit of giving continues to touch lives every single day."

A Self-Made Legacy

While Dr. Rabinder Nath's great-great-grandfather, Rai Bahadur Mul Raj, was a towering figure in India's freedom movement—founding Punjab National Bank and the D.A.V. College movement—the family's fortunes did not pass down untouched. Through the upheavals of partition and the passage of time, the family rebuilt from scratch, relying not on inherited wealth, but on education and determination.

Education as Foundation

Dr. Rabinder Nath pursued excellence through education, earning his dental degree (B.D.S.) from Osmania Medical College, where he served as Chairman of the Dental Students Federation of India. He completed advanced training in oral surgery at Lucknow Dental College (1974) and earned M.R.S.H. from the Royal College of Surgeons, England.

Academic Leadership: First Union President at A.V. College

Built from Scratch

Every achievement—his dental practice, his books, his social impact—was earned through his own hard work, not inherited privilege

His Own Legacy

Dr. Rabinder Nath became beloved across Hyderabad on his own merits, creating a legacy that is entirely his own

The ancestral story connects him to a proud history, but Dr. Rabinder Nath's achievements are a testament to his personal dedication, brilliance, and compassion. He proved that true legacy is not inherited—it is earned.

Community Impact Through Healthcare

Beyond his dental practice, Dr. Rabinder Nath dedicated his life to serving rural and underserved communities

Portadent Mobile Dental Unit

Pioneered mobile dental healthcare by establishing "Portadent," bringing professional dental care to rural populations who had no access to dental services.

Rani Nath Memorial Hospital

Founded in 1992 at Shamirpet, this rural hospital provided completely free treatment and medicines, serving over 80,000 patients from underserved communities.

Medical Research Contributions

Conducted successful field trials on Mefenamic acid (anti-inflammatory), leading to the development of Tromaril drug by R.R. Labs, Hyderabad.

Mahabubnagar Rural Hospital

Established another rural dental hospital in Mahabubnagar district, extending quality dental care to remote areas.

Dr. Rabinder Nath: The Man and His Impact

Dr. Rabinder Nath was more than a dental surgeon—he was a social worker, pioneer, and beloved figure across Hyderabad. Known by families throughout the city, Dr. Nath dedicated his life to serving others with compassion and dignity.

Dental Surgeon

Practiced dentistry with care and excellence, treating patients across all walks of life

Social Worker

Dedicated countless hours to community service and social causes

Author & Pioneer

Published 7+ books, including works on Punjabi history and culture

His well-wishers and the community came together to create this foundation, ensuring that his spirit of service would continue to uplift and support those in need for generations to come.

The Legacy Continues

Three Generations of Medical Service

Dr. Prem Nath

India's First Qualified Dental Surgeon

Pioneer who established dental surgery as a professional medical discipline in India

Dr. Upendra Nath

Personal Dental Physician to the Nizam of Hyderabad

Served as Royal Surgeon to the Nizam and subsequent Governors of Andhra Pradesh. Founded Nath Dental & Oral Surgery Clinic in 1956.

Dr. Rabinder Nath (1951-present)

Pioneering Rural Healthcare & Philanthropy

Revolutionized rural dental care through mobile units, established free hospitals serving 80,000+ patients, and created the foundation that continues his legacy of compassionate service.

Nath Dental & Oral Surgery Clinic

Dr. Rabinder Nath's dental practice continues to serve the community at the historic Nath Dental & Oral Surgery Clinic, established in 1956 by his father Dr. Upender Nath.

  • Address: 3-5-809, Nath Nivas, King Koti Road, Hyderguda, Hyderabad - 500029
  • Landmark: Beside Bahar Cafe, Basheerbagh
  • Currently Led by: Dr. Niharika Nath (BDS, 26+ years experience)
  • Services: General Dentistry, Oral Surgery, Dental Restoration

Dr. Narendra Nath, Dr. Rabinder Nath's younger brother, also continues the family tradition of medical service as a renowned physician in Hyderabad, operating his clinic at the same Nath Nivas complex.

Our Approach: Partnership for Impact

Leveraging innovative infrastructure to maximize community reach and sustainable impact

Strategic Partnership with LooCafe

At DRNF, we believe that maximizing social impact requires thinking beyond traditional charity models. Rather than building separate infrastructure from scratch, we strategically partner with LooCafe—founded in 2018, now operating 270+ community-accessible facilities across Hyderabad.

Our partnership with LooCafe began after their establishment in 2018, allowing us to place our free chai counters where people naturally gather, ensuring our services reach those who need them most. By utilizing existing infrastructure, we can focus our resources directly on community service rather than construction and maintenance.

The collaboration is documented in LooCafe's Toilet Tales, showcasing how community-driven NGO initiatives like ours can create meaningful impact through smart partnerships with infrastructure providers.

Proven Model: Successful Pilot Program

In 2022-2023, we successfully piloted our Free Chai Counter program across 30 locations, validating our approach and refining our operational model. The results exceeded our expectations:

Now serving 500-600 people daily with free chai and snacks
Created 70+ sustainable employment opportunities
Built strong relationships with local communities
Demonstrated scalable, sustainable model

Current Status: We now operate 2 permanent free chai counters, with plans to expand across the network as resources allow. Our focus remains on quality of service and community impact, ensuring every cup served comes with dignity and compassion.
