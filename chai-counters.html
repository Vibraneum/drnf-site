Free Chai Counters

"No One Should Sleep Hungry" - Our mission through 365 days of free tea and snacks

Currently Operating

2 Free Chai Counters are currently active in Hyderabad, serving the community with free tea, snacks, and support for those in need.

Locations: KBR Park, LV Prasad, and Basavatarakam junctions

Serving: 500-600 people daily with free chai and biscuits

Operating Hours: 9 AM to 9 PM, 365 days a year

Launch Date: July 2021

Our Chai Counter Locations

Partnership with LooCafe

Our free chai counters are strategically implemented through our partnership with LooCafe.com, leveraging their extensive network of 270+ locations across Hyderabad.

The mission is beautifully documented in LooCafe's Toilet Tales, showcasing how community-driven initiatives like ours create meaningful impact through innovative partnerships.

LooCafe is more than just a network of public facilities - it's a revolutionary social impact model backed by the Gates Foundation that has created 1,000+ jobs, empowered street vendors to become entrepreneurs, and provides sustainable infrastructure for community services like our free chai counters.
