Our Mission

DRNF was founded in 2020 to honor the legacy of Dr. Rabinder Nath - a pioneering dental surgeon and lifelong philanthropist who dedicated his life to serving the community.

Today, DRNF serves 500-600 people daily through free chai counters, has created 70+ jobs, and continues to support frontline workers and the community with compassion and dedication.

Key Financial Principles

Lean Operations

By partnering with LooCafe and leveraging existing infrastructure, we keep overhead costs minimal—typically under 5% of total spending.

Job Creation as Impact

70+ jobs created means employment costs are program impact, not overhead. Every worker hired lifts a family out of poverty.

Ixora Group Support

Many administrative costs are covered by Ixora Corporate Services, allowing more donated funds to go directly to programs.

How We Measure Impact

Evidence-based approach to tracking and reporting our work

Data Collection

  • Daily Counts: Counter staff record cups served and beneficiaries
  • Financial Tracking: All expenses logged through Ixora's accounting system
  • Employment Records: HR data tracks jobs created and sustained

Calculation Methods

  • People Served: Daily cups ÷ 2 (avg 2 cups/person) = unique individuals
  • Cost/Cup: Total supply cost ÷ total cups served
  • Jobs Created: Direct headcount employed by DRNF programs

Verification & Auditing

  • Monthly Reviews: Leadership reviews all program data monthly
  • External Audit: Ixora's annual financial audit includes DRNF
  • Public Reporting: Impact data updated on website quarterly

Commitment to Accuracy

We report conservative estimates whenever exact data is unavailable. For example, "500-600 people daily" reflects the range we can confidently document. We never inflate numbers to look better—transparency builds trust, and trust creates lasting impact.
