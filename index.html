Dr. Rabinder Nath Foundation

Who We Are

Created in the memory of the Late Vijayshree Dr. Rabinder Nath, the Dr. Rabinder Nath Foundation honors his legacy as a Dental Surgeon, Social Worker, and Pioneer in Hyderabad and across India.

A beloved figure cherished by families across the city, our foundation was established by his well-wishers and community to continue his mission of service and compassion. Today, we serve through free chai counters, community support programs, educational workshops, and environmental initiatives.
