Early Life & Education

Born on February 15, 1951 in Hyderabad, Dr. Rabinder Nath was the son of Dr. Upendra Nath, personal dental physician to the Nizam of Hyderabad, and grandson of Dr. Prem Nath, India's first qualified dental surgeon. Despite this prestigious lineage, the family had lost much through the upheavals of partition, and Dr. Rabinder Nath's achievements were built through education, determination, and merit.

Medical Career & Dental Practice

Dr. Rabinder Nath joined the family practice at Nath Dental & Oral Surgery Clinic (founded 1956 by his father Dr. Upendra Nath) and expanded it into a renowned center for dental care in Hyderabad. His practice philosophy combined clinical excellence with compassionate care, treating patients from all walks of life.

Research & Innovation

Pharmaceutical Research

Mefenamic Acid Field Trials

Conducted pioneering clinical field trials on Mefenamic acid, a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used for pain relief and inflammation management.

Following successful trials, R.R. Labs Hyderabad developed and commercialized "Tromaril", bringing affordable pain management to millions of patients across India.

This research contributed to making effective pain relief accessible to the masses at affordable prices.

Mobile Healthcare Innovation

Portadent Mobile Dental Unit

Established "Portadent", a pioneering mobile dental unit designed to bring advanced dental care directly to rural and underserved populations.

The mobile unit was equipped with:

  • Portable dental chairs
  • Sterilization equipment
  • Basic surgical instruments
  • Emergency dental supplies
  • Educational materials

Reached villages and communities that had never seen a dentist before.

Rural Healthcare Legacy

Dr. Rabinder Nath's most enduring contribution was establishing healthcare infrastructure in rural areas where none existed. His commitment to serving the poor and underprivileged went beyond urban practice to create lasting institutions in underserved regions.

Mahabubnagar Rural Dental Hospital

Serving one of Telangana's most backward districts

Established a dedicated rural dental hospital in Mahabubnagar district, one of the most economically disadvantaged regions of Telangana at the time. The hospital focused specifically on oral health, which was critically neglected in rural areas.

Services Provided:

  • Free dental consultations and examinations
  • Tooth extractions and emergency dental care
  • Preventive oral health education
  • School dental health programs
  • Community awareness campaigns

Many rural communities in Mahabubnagar had never seen a dentist before this hospital opened. It addressed critical oral health issues that often went untreated, causing severe pain and health complications.

Family & Three Generations of Medical Service

The Nath family represents three generations of pioneering medical service to India, each building upon the foundation laid by the previous generation while forging their own path of innovation and service.

Dr. Prem Nath

First Generation
India's First Qualified Dental Surgeon

Dr. Prem Nath holds the distinction of being recognized as India's first qualified dental surgeon, pioneering the profession of dentistry in the country during the early 20th century. His groundbreaking work established dentistry as a respected medical field in India and paved the way for modern dental education and practice.

Dr. Upendra Nath (Dr. Upender Nath)

Second Generation • Founded Nath Dental Clinic (1956)
Personal Dental Physician to the Nizam of Hyderabad

Following in his father's footsteps, Dr. Upendra Nath became a renowned oral surgeon and served as the personal dental physician to the Nizam of Hyderabad and subsequent state governors. In 1956, he founded Nath Dental & Oral Surgery Clinic at Nath Nivas, Hyderguda, establishing what would become a family legacy of medical service spanning nearly 70 years.

Legacy: "His work and fame is still remembered by his patients and friends" who continue to speak of his skill and dedication decades later.

Dr. Rabinder Nath

Third Generation • Born February 15, 1951
Pioneering Rural Healthcare & Philanthropy

Dr. Rabinder Nath expanded the family legacy beyond urban practice to serve rural and underprivileged populations. He established Rani Nath Memorial Hospital (serving 80,000+ patients with free treatment), pioneered mobile dental healthcare through Portadent, conducted pharmaceutical research, and earned international recognition for his social service work.

Personal Life

Dr. Rabinder Nath was married to Mrs. Neelam Nath. Together they had two children who continue the family's tradition of service and innovation.

Son: Abhishek Nath - Founder of LooCafe (WASH organization) and Dr. Rabinder Nath Foundation (established 2016), carrying forward the family's commitment to social service through innovative community programs including free chai counters and sanitation initiatives.
